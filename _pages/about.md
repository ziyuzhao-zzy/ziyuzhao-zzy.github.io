I am Ziyu Zhao, a second-year Master’s student at the School of Software, Tsinghua University. I received my B.Eng. degree from the Fangongxiu Honors College, Beijing University of Technology.

I am currently an intern at the Image and Multimodal Laboratory, JD Explore Academy, where I focus on video generation. Previously, I interned at the Data-TNS-ALGO team of ByteDance (Nov 2023 – Jun 2024), working on Video Large Language Models (Video LLMs).
